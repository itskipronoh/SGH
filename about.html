Cancellation Policy

We understand that sometimes plans change. To ensure the best service for all our clients, we have the following cancellation guidelines:

  • Free Cancellation: Up to 3 hours before your appointment
  • Late Cancellation Fee: Up to 30% of booking value for cancellations less than 3 hours
  • No-Show Policy: Bookings automatically cancelled 1 hour after scheduled time

Cancellation fees only apply when policy terms are violated.

Reschedule Policy
